Tiramisu

Description

Literally translates to "pick me up". A world known amazing italian dessert.

Preparation: 30 minutes

Cooling time: 3 H

Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 300g (about 30) Savoiardi biscuits
  • 500g of Mascarpone
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 100g of granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of Amaretto
  • cocoa powder

Preparation

  1. First of all make the coffee. An espresso coffee machine is best but a mokka can be used as well. Add the Amaretto and set it aside.
  2. Separate the egg white from yolks. Set aside the yolks and whip the egg whites until stiff. If the egg white donâ€™t turn when you turn the bowl over, you got it.
  3. Now in a bowl, beat the egg yolks with sugar until light and smooth, 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. In the meantime, pour the mascarpone cheese into a bowl and work it with a spoon to make it softer. Mascarpone must be of excellent quality, creamy and thick. When the yolks are ready add the mascarpone.
  5. Slowly whip the mascarpone cream for 2 to 3 minutes. Now add the stiffly beaten egg whites.
  6. Mix with a wooden spoon, from bottom up. Mix slowly until smooth and creamy
  7. Now it's time for the savoiardi. Dip the biscuits quickly into the coffee (1-2 seconds max). Then arrange the savoiardi in the casserole.
  8. Arrange them so that they cover the bottom of the casserole. Then spread the mascarpone cream over the ladyfingers.
  9. Add another layer of savoiardi and then top with more mascarpone cream. If you are making the last layer, spread the mascarpone generously.
  10. Finally, sprinkle with cocoa powder. You can even add dark chocolate chips, if you like.
  11. Let it rest for at least 3 hours before serving.
